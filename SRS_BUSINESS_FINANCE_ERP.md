## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Project Overview
The Business Finance Management System is a comprehensive ERP application designed specifically for IT companies to manage financial operations with bank-grade accuracy and investor-ready reporting capabilities.

### 1.2 Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Flutter Web, Material 3, GetX, Go Router
- **Backend:** Appwrite (Database, Authentication, Storage, Realtime)
- **Charts:** FL Chart
- **Export:** PDF generation, Excel export
- **Architecture:** Clean Architecture, Repository Pattern
